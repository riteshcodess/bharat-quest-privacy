Section 01

Information We Collect

Quick Summary

Bharat Quest operates without user registration or mandatory cloud accounts. All your game progression, coins, and unlocks are stored locally on your device.

Unlike online multiplayer games requiring mandatory email verification, social media logins, or continuous background syncing to external servers, Bharat Quest is designed as an offline-first mobile game.

When you launch and play the app, all progression metrics, scores, and rewards are created directly inside your device's private local memory (`localStorage` and native device file storage). Specifically, our app generates and maintains the following locally:

🏆

Quiz & Accuracy Records

Tracks your state completions, historical score accuracy, and best times across Indian culture, history, and mythology.

🪙

Economy & Streaks

Stores your earned coins, rare gems, daily login streak multipliers, and claimed daily challenge rewards.

🎴

Collection Inventory

Maintains your unlocked legend cards, state emblems, achievement badges, custom avatars, and home state selection.

⚙️

App Preferences

Saves your audio effects volume, background music toggle, haptic vibration feedback preferences, and display name.

Section 02

Advertising & AdMob

Quick Summary

We partner with Google AdMob to display optional rewarded ads and occasional interstitial breaks to keep the game 100% free.

To ensure Bharat Quest remains free to download and accessible across all regions, the application incorporates third-party advertisements delivered by Google AdMob (`@capacitor-community/admob`), a premier mobile advertising service operated by Google LLC.

When an ad is requested and displayed on your screen, Google AdMob and its certified advertising network partners automatically process specific technical identifiers and diagnostic metrics to deliver relevant, frequency-capped advertisements.

  • ✓
    Google Advertising ID (AD_ID): A unique, user-resettable identifier provided by your Android OS. It is utilized exclusively for serving relevant ad units, capping how often you see the same ad, and attributing rewarded ad completions.
  • ✓
    Device Technical Specifications: Your device model, Android OS version, screen resolution, and basic network connectivity status (e.g., Wi-Fi vs. Cellular data).
  • ✓
    Diagnostic Crash Reports: Anonymous technical performance logs if an advertisement fails to load or causes an unexpected application freeze during ad playback.

Your Opt-Out Control: You can reset your Android Advertising ID or opt out of personalized advertisements anytime by opening your phone's Settings → Google → Ads and adjusting your device-level privacy preferences.

Section 03

Storage & Data Deletion

Quick Summary

You have 100% control over your save data. You can export/backup files locally or wipe all progress instantly using the in-app Reset button.

Because your progress is stored on your device rather than on remote central servers, you possess absolute ownership and control over your data. We have built comprehensive self-service tools right into the game:

⚠️

In-App "Reset Progress"

Open the in-app Settings Sheet and tap the red Reset Progress button. This instantly and permanently wipes all local storage data, resetting your account to zero immediately.

📂

Native Save File Export

Download a backup `.json` file of your complete game progress straight to your phone's Documents folder anytime using the Download Save File button.

Section 06

Children's Privacy

Bharat Quest is an educational exploration mobile game celebrating Indian heritage suitable for general audiences. However, we do not knowingly solicit, collect, or store personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

Because our game functions without registration forms, real-name requirements, or server accounts, no personal identity details are ever collected from young players. Moreover, our Google AdMob integration strictly complies with applicable child safety laws including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and the Google Play Families Policy where applicable.
