01

Information We Collect & Store Locally

Unlike online multiplayer applications that require user registration, email verification, cloud passwords, or continuous background syncing to remote servers, Bharat Quest is designed primarily as an offline-first mobile game.

When you launch and play Bharat Quest, all core gameplay progression data is generated and maintained locally inside your device's internal memory (`localStorage` and native Android file system). Specifically, the app maintains the following local data:

🏆

Quiz & Accuracy Records

Stores completed quiz scores, best accuracy per state, and historical performance across Indian history and mythology.

🪙

Economy & Streaks

Maintains your earned coins, rare gems, daily login streak counters, and claimed daily challenge rewards.

🎴

Collection Inventory

Tracks unlocked legend cards, state emblems, achievement badges, player avatars, and selected themes.

⚙️

App Settings

Saves your audio sound effects volume, background music toggle, haptic vibration preferences, and display name.

Local-First Guarantee: None of your personal identity, contact lists, photos, microphone recordings, or gameplay statistics are transmitted to our servers or sold to data brokers.

02

Advertising & Google AdMob

To keep Bharat Quest free to download and accessible across all Indian states and global regions, the application incorporates third-party advertisements delivered by Google AdMob (`@capacitor-community/admob`), a mobile advertising network operated by Google LLC.

When advertisements (such as optional rewarded video ads for extra game coins or interstitial breaks) are requested and rendered on your screen, Google AdMob automatically processes certain technical device identifiers and app metrics:

  • ✓
    Google Advertising ID (AD_ID): A unique, user-resettable identifier assigned by your Android OS. It is utilized exclusively for serving relevant ads, frequency capping (preventing repetitive ad views), and ad attribution.
  • ✓
    Technical Device Characteristics: Your device model, Android OS version, screen resolution, system language, and network connection status (Wi-Fi vs. Cellular data).
  • ✓
    Ad Diagnostic & Crash Metrics: Anonymous performance reports if an ad fails to load or causes an unexpected application freeze during rendering.

Your Opt-Out Control: You can reset your Android Advertising ID or opt out of personalized advertisements at any time by navigating to your phone's Settings → Google → Ads and adjusting your device-level privacy controls.

03

Storage & Data Deletion

Because all your game progression is stored locally inside your device's memory, you maintain 100% control over your data. You have full authority to manage, backup, or permanently erase your progress right from within the app:

⚠️

In-App "Reset Progress" Option

To instantly delete all local data, open Bharat Quest and navigate to the Settings Sheet. Tap the red Reset Progress button. This immediately wipes all stored coins, unlocked cards, streaks, and quiz history from your phone, resetting the game completely.

📂

Native Save File Export

You can also download a backup `.json` file of your complete game progression straight to your device's Documents folder anytime using the Download Save File button inside Settings.

06

Children's Privacy

Bharat Quest is an educational exploration game celebrating Indian culture and history suitable for general audiences. However, we do not knowingly solicit, collect, or store personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

Because the game operates without user registration or server-side accounts, no names, birthdates, or contact details are ever collected from young players. Furthermore, third-party advertising via Google AdMob is configured to adhere to applicable child protection regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and the Google Play Families Policy where applicable.
