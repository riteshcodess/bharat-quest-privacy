Privacy Policy

We believe in transparent, privacy-first gaming. This document explains what information is collected, how your progress is saved locally, and how advertisements are delivered inside Bharat Quest.

Application Name: Bharat Quest
Target Platform: Android (Google Play)
Effective Date: July 19, 2026
01

Information We Collect

Bharat Quest is designed with privacy as a foundational principle. Unlike online multiplayer games requiring account registration, cloud syncing, or mandatory personal data collection, our application operates primarily on your device.

When you play Bharat Quest, all core gameplay data and progression metrics are created and stored directly in your device's local memory (`localStorage` and native device storage). Specifically, the app generates and maintains the following locally:

🏆 Quiz Progress & Accuracy

Records completed quizzes, scores, best accuracy per state, and historical performance across Indian history, geography, culture, and mythology.

🪙 Economy & Rewards

Tracks your earned coins, gems, daily login streak counters, and claimed daily challenge bonuses.

🎴 Collection & Unlocks

Stores unlocked legend cards, discovery cards, achievement badges, player avatars, and custom theme selections.

⚙️ Custom Preferences

Saves your audio sound effects volume, background music toggle, haptic feedback settings, and chosen display name.

🔒
Local-First Privacy Guarantee

None of your personal identity, contact lists, location data, or gameplay statistics are transmitted to our remote servers or sold to data brokers. Your quest remains entirely yours.

02

Advertising & Monetization

To keep Bharat Quest free to download and play, the application incorporates third-party advertisements delivered by Google AdMob (`@capacitor-community/admob`), a mobile advertising service provided by Google LLC.

When advertisements (such as rewarded video ads for extra game coins or interstitial breaks) are requested and displayed, Google AdMob and its advertising partners automatically collect and process certain technical device identifiers and app usage metrics.

According to Google's policies and standard industry practices, AdMob may collect and utilize the following information:

  • Google Advertising ID (AD_ID): A unique, resettable identifier assigned by the Android operating system used for serving relevant ads, frequency capping (preventing repetitive ad views), and ad attribution.
  • Device Technical Characteristics: Device model, operating system version, screen resolution, system language, and general network connection type (Wi-Fi vs. Cellular).
  • Ad Interaction Metrics: Information regarding whether an ad was viewed, clicked, or dismissed, as well as general app performance and crash diagnostics related to ad rendering.
ℹ️
Your Advertising Preferences & Opt-Out

You can reset your Android Advertising ID or opt out of personalized advertisements at any time by navigating to your phone's Settings → Google → Ads and adjusting your device-level privacy controls.

03

Data Storage & User Control

All game progression data is stored locally inside your application's secure data directory on your Android device. We do not maintain centralized user databases, nor do we require you to register an account with an email address or password.

Because you are in complete control of your local data, you have complete authority to manage, export, or permanently erase your progress whenever you choose right from within the app:

🗑️ In-App Data Deletion

You can permanently erase all local save data, coins, unlocked cards, and stats by opening the in-app Settings Sheet and tapping the red Reset Progress button. This action wipes the storage cleanly and resets the game to its initial state immediately.

📦 Local Save Export & Import

You can download a complete backup of your game progress directly to your phone's Documents folder using the Download Save File option, or restore it anytime using Upload Save File.

04

Third-Party Services

While Bharat Quest does not directly collect or share personal information, the application relies on select third-party libraries and SDKs to provide core technical functionalities. These service providers operate under their own independent privacy terms:

  • Google AdMob (Google LLC): Used for delivering in-game advertisements. For detailed information on how Google processes data when you use AdMob applications, please review the Google Privacy Policy and How Google uses data from apps.
  • Android OS & Google Play Services: Required for application distribution, native haptic vibration feedback (`@capacitor/haptics`), and system-level app state management (`@capacitor/app`).
05

Children's Privacy

Bharat Quest is an educational exploration game about Indian culture and history suitable for general audiences. However, we do not knowingly solicit, collect, or store personally identifiable information from children under the age of 13.

Because the game operates without user registration or server-side accounts, no names, birthdates, or contact details are ever collected from young players. Furthermore, third-party advertising via Google AdMob is configured to adhere to applicable child protection regulations including COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and the Google Play Families Policy where applicable.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information through a third-party channel, please contact us promptly so that we can take appropriate action.
